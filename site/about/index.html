Who we are

Pete’s Plumbing is a locally owned and operated plumbing company serving Armonk and the nearby Westchester County communities. We have over 15 years of experience helping homeowners and small businesses with everyday plumbing repairs, emergency plumbing, water heaters, drain cleaning, leak repairs, sewer line problems, septic-connected plumbing, fixture installations, sump pumps, and light commercial plumbing.

We live and work in the same towns we serve. That is not a marketing line — it is the reason we care whether you would call us again, and the reason we would rather tell you a repair can wait than sell you one you do not need.

How we work

Most of what people remember about a plumber is not the pipe. It is whether they answered, showed up when they said they would, explained the problem clearly, and left the place clean. That is what we try to get right every time.

Our approach on any job is the same. We diagnose the actual cause rather than the nearest symptom, because water travels and the wet patch is rarely where the problem started. We explain what we found and lay out the options, including the cheaper one. We give you the price before we start, and if we open something up and find the job is different, we stop and talk to you rather than pressing on. Then we do the work and take the mess with us.

You can see the full range of what we actually do day to day across Westchester homes and small businesses, from a dripping faucet to a failed sewer line.

What we are known for

Honest recommendations

We diagnose first and tell you plainly whether a repair beats a replacement, and when something can wait. No pressure, no unnecessary upsells.

Clear communication

You will understand what is wrong and what we propose to do about it, in plain language rather than jargon, before any work begins.

Clean workmanship

Floors protected, work done tidily, and the mess taken with us. An already-bad day should not end with you cleaning up after us.

Reliable service

We answer the phone, show up when promised, and stand behind the work. Same-day service is often available, and we handle emergency plumbing too.

Built for Westchester homes

Working in one county for years means we know what tends to be behind a problem before we arrive. Older neighbourhoods with original cast iron waste stacks and clay sewer laterals. Mature trees over those laterals. A real mix of municipal sewer and septic within a few miles. Finished basements where a small leak becomes an expensive one quickly. And winters cold enough that pipes in crawlspaces, garages, and exterior walls need thinking about every year.

We have particular experience with Armonk-area housing: septic-connected plumbing, finished basements, sump pumps, older plumbing systems, and the cold-weather pipe problems that come with our winters. It is the difference between guessing and knowing where to look first.

Licensed, insured, and local

Credentials matter in this trade, so to be clear about ours:

  • Licensed plumber
  • Westchester County licensed plumber
  • Insured
  • Residential & light commercial plumbing

Being a licensed Westchester County plumber is also what allows our work to be permitted and inspected properly — which matters more than people expect when a house is later sold or an insurance claim is made. When something genuinely needs how we handle emergency calls, you are dealing with someone accountable and local, not a call centre.

Where we work

We are based in Armonk and cover Westchester County, including Pleasantville, Mount Kisco, Yonkers, Bedford, and Scarsdale. If you are near those and not sure whether you are in range, just ask when you call.
